Give your AI Agent one sentence, and it automatically generates a complete set of livestream overlay graphics.

For example, just tell your Agent:

> Help me create a Fila livestream overlay. Main products: Cat Paw Shoes and Mars Shoes. 150 off every 1100. Buy 2 pairs, get a co-branded bucket hat. Member-exclusive limited gift box packaging.

You can also upload a product photo and let AI recognize and generate from it.

In about 1 minute, you'll get a full suite: background, title banner, presenter character, promo card, product display shelf, benefits bar, and a composite preview. Each component is a separate transparent PNG layer, ready for further editing and repositioning.
